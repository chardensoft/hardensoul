About Me


My name is Cintia and I love photography! I was born in Fortaleza, Brazil and moved to the United States in 2018. After being here for a year, I met my husband and in June 2020 we got married in the Provo City Center Temple. We love our life together and just bought our first home!

I picked up photography from a kind neighbor we met in our neighborhood. I have absolutely loved it ever since! I do a ton of nature photography but I can do just about anything under the sun. I've don a good number of weddings and other life events.

Outside of photography, I love to play video games and cook. Learning to make American food has been a journey and my husband, Ben, is my taste tester. My favorite video game is Overwatch and we have a whole gaming room set up in our house. We have a great time.
